Edge AI & Robotic Systems

July 1 – July 8, 2026

The ACM India Summer School 2026 on Edge AI and Robotics will provide a focused overview of intelligent edge systems that integrate AI with modern robotic platforms. The program will cover edge computing architectures, hardware accelerators, real-time perception and control, and co-optimization of machine learning models for performance, power, and accuracy on resource-constrained devices. Participants will also explore federated learning, generative AI/LLMs, and autonomous agents deployed on edge and robotic systems across applications such as smart mobility, drones, industrial automation, and smart agriculture. Through lectures, hands-on labs, and expert talks, the school will equip participants with practical skills to design and deploy intelligent edge-robotic systems for real-world applications.

Recommended Background

  • Foundational knowledge in Data Science, Machine Learning, or AI basics.
  • Understanding of Computer Systems architecture.
  • Familiarity with Embedded Systems and IoT programming concepts.
